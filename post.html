Why You Should Switch to a Checking Account That Offers Interest

Getting interest is now on the table with these new checking accounts

Posted by Paxton Hansen on April 16, 2023

Are you tired of your traditional checking account sucking the life out of your hard-earned cash? Do you want your money to actually make money for you? Well, listen up college kids, because I'm about to tell you how to get more bang for your buck with a checking account that pays interest.

Now, I know what you're thinking, "Interest? In a checking account? Is that even possible?" The answer is yes! And the best part? It's easy to switch to an interest-bearing checking account that can actually help you save money and grow your wealth.

Two great options on the market right now are SoFi's 1.2% APY account and Axos's 3.3% APY account. That's right, you can earn real interest on your checking account balance and watch your money grow.

Why Make the Switch?

So why should you make the switch? First of all, it's a no-brainer. You're going to have a checking account anyway, so why not make it work for you? Plus, you'll have the peace of mind knowing that your money is growing, even if it's just a little bit.

By Randy Glasbergen

Secondly, you're in college now, which means you're starting to take your finances more seriously. With an interest-bearing checking account, you'll learn the importance of saving and investing early on. You'll be able to see firsthand how compound interest works, and how it can turn small contributions into big savings over time.

But let's be real, the main reason you're probably considering an interest-bearing checking account is for the extra cash. Whether you're saving up for spring break or trying to pay off student loans, every little bit helps. And with Axos's 3.3% APY account, you'll be earning almost triple the interest rate of SoFi's 1.2% APY account. That's some serious dough!

So, what are you waiting for? Make the switch to an interest-bearing checking account today and watch your money grow. With SoFi's and Axos's competitive interest rates, you'll be one step closer to achieving your financial goals. Who knew personal finance could be this exciting?
